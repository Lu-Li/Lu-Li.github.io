Correlation between Various Types of Service

      Various types of service are closely connected to each other today. A crash of one service can cause chain reaction which result in a series of other services crash down.

      The crash of AWS from Amazon on Christmas Eve of 2013 cause Netflix’s downloads service break down. iCloud’s crash on October 10th , 2012 cause problems on iMessage, Apps Store, Game Center. More seriously, the downtime of Google service during from 6:50 to 6:55 on August 17th 2013 led to the 40% decline of global traffics, including YouTube, Google Drive and Gmail. This means Google service have some influences on the other services.

      This project is to propose a model to evaluation the correlation between each service to help built a more reliable service structure, in which we can improve communication network’s stability. The data set we used are provided by China Unicom and covers a large user base over a rather long time period, as well as include data on connection opportunities encountered24 hours a day.
